Government Registration, Licensing, and Trademarks

In British Columbia, the Private Training Institutions Branch (PTIB) of the Ministry of Advanced Education maintains the bylaw and regulations. Programs are not offered until appropriate approvals have been granted by PTIB and any governing body if required.
