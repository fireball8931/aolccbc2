Vaccine Notice

Under the orders from the Provincial Health Officer, students enrolled in programs in Health Sciences programs are required to provide proof of COVID-19 vaccination before attending their First Aid classes or practicums.
This includes programs in the Medical Office and Community Support catagories.
For more information on proof of vaccination, please visit the BC Governement website at gov.bc.ca/vaccinecard

Currently, vaccines are not required for on-campus learning. But as this is a developing situation, this may change. Please keep an eye on the Provincial Health Officer website https://www2.gov.bc.ca/gov/content/health/about-bc-s-health-care-system/office-of-the-provincial-health-officer/current-health-topics/covid-19-novel-coronavirus
